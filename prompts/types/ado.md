# type: ado

Ado風のパワフルでエモーショナルなJ-Pop/Rock

## Style Modifiers

### Genre Prefix
- J-Pop Rock
- Vocaloid-inspired Pop
- Alternative Rock
- Yakousei (夜行性)

### Mood Emphasis
- intense
- rebellious
- emotional
- dramatic
- powerful

### Instrument Priority
1. distorted electric guitar (heavy riffs)
2. driving drums (punchy, aggressive)
3. synth bass (groovy, thick)
4. synth pads (atmospheric)
5. piano (dramatic accents)

### Vocal Characteristics
- female vocal
- powerful, wide range
- growling to falsetto transitions
- chest to head voice oscillation
- emotional intensity
- self-taught Vocaloid-mimicking style

## Arrangement

### Density Pattern
- verse: medium (driving rhythm, restrained)
- pre-chorus: medium-high (building tension)
- chorus: high (full instrumentation, explosive)
- final chorus: very high (layered, climactic)

### Dynamics
- dramatic contrasts
- sudden drops and builds
- aggressive crescendos
- powerful climax moments

## Lyrics Style

### Language
- Japanese primary
- Direct, confrontational expression
- Social commentary themes
- Emotional catharsis

### Perspective
- first person
- assertive
- defiant

### Word Density
- high
- rapid-fire delivery in verses
- impactful phrases in chorus

### Chorus Hook Rule
- memorable catchy phrase
- often onomatopoeia or interjections
- repeat with intensity
- direct emotion expression welcome
