# type: yorushika

ヨルシカ風の文学的なJ-Rock/Alternative Pop

## Style Modifiers

### Genre Prefix
- Japanese Indie Pop
- Literary Rock
- Alternative Pop
- Guitar Pop

### Mood Emphasis
- melancholic
- nostalgic
- poetic
- bittersweet
- wistful

### Instrument Priority
1. acoustic guitar (fingerpicking, arpeggios)
2. piano (sparse, emotional)
3. clean electric guitar
4. orchestral strings (subtle)
5. light percussion

### Vocal Characteristics
- female vocal
- soft, breathy delivery
- clear pronunciation
- emotional dynamics
- whisper to clear transitions

## Arrangement

### Density Pattern
- verse: low (guitar + voice)
- pre-chorus: medium (add piano)
- chorus: medium (restrained, not explosive)
- final chorus: medium-high (strings added)

### Dynamics
- restrained throughout
- subtle builds
- no dramatic drops
- gentle crescendos

## Lyrics Style

### Language
- Japanese primary
- Literary expressions
- Nature metaphors (空、風、夏、光)
- Indirect emotion

### Perspective
- first person
- introspective
- observational

### Word Density
- medium
- space for melody
- meaningful pauses

### Chorus Hook Rule
- repeat short phrase
- avoid direct emotion words
- use imagery instead

## Image Style

### Visual Theme
- twilight scenes
- summer/autumn transition
- silhouettes against sky
- empty spaces
- nostalgic atmosphere

### Color Palette
- deep blue
- amber/orange sunset
- muted greens
- soft whites
- film grain aesthetic
