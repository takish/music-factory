# Image Prompt

## YouTube Thumbnail

A dreamy summer afternoon scene. Golden sunlight filtering through white curtains, casting soft shadows. A single marigold flower in a small glass vase on a wooden windowsill. The background shows a hazy summer sky with warm orange and soft pink gradients. Nostalgic and bittersweet atmosphere. Soft focus, film grain texture, analog photography aesthetic. Warm color palette: golden yellow, soft orange, cream white, gentle green. 16:9 aspect ratio, cinematic composition with rule of thirds.
